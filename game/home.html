Mind the Fog

Welcome! Mind the Fog is a two man production, coming out of the suburbs of Boston, MA.

Alex and Matt have spent a little under a year putting together this game and while it has its quirks still, we think it is a a suitable alpha release.

If you find any bugs, big or small, please log them on our GitHub issue tracker, and we'll try to get to them at earliest convenience (as full time students, it could take a while!). Better yet, feel free to try to fix the bug yourself and submit a pull request!

Now, without further ado, we present Mind the Fog. Enjoy!
